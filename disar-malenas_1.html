The Chronicle of Disar Malenas
I — Before Hell Whispered His Name

Long before Hell ever whispered his name, Disar Malenas was human.

On Earth, he lived a quiet, overworked soul buried beneath deadlines, obligations, and endless routines. He gave everything to survive… and almost nothing to himself. Friends were rare. Family time was distant. But the few people who truly understood him kept him standing.

He wasn't unhappy.
Just… slowly disappearing.

II — The Impossible Encounter

Everything changed during a single, impossible encounter. A mysterious pink-haired demon crossed his path — someone who didn't hunt humans, didn't threaten him, and didn't lie about what Hell truly was.

She showed him a hidden truth: Hell was not only punishment. It was also a battlefield for broken souls who refused to stay powerless. Her goal was simple — to gather those who had been crushed by life… and teach them how to rise.

He chose to follow her.
Not for power — but for purpose.

III — The Corruption of Humanity

The transformation did not erase his humanity. It corrupted it. Warped it.

Strength, speed, and perception awakened inside him — but so did something far more dangerous.

Mind Reading
Perception of thought, intent, and hidden truths
Hyper-Observation
Instinctive awareness spanning all realms
Extreme Agility
Raw physical power refined into silent precision
Realm Teleportation
Instant transit across the boundaries of existence
IV — Three Identities, One Fracture

The greatest change was not physical. It was spiritual.

The First
The Human
Still alive inside his heart. The part that remembers compassion.
The Second
The Demon
The Reaper commander he has become. Duty and dominion.
The Third
The Angel
Sealed and suppressed. Its absence fractures everything.

The suppression of that angelic side causes constant mental distortion. Thoughts overlap. Emotions echo. Memories arrive from lives he never fully lived.

Trying to balance all three realms fractures his mind more than any battlefield ever could.

V — The Promotion That Was a Leash

Through countless operations in Hell's internal wars, Disar rose quickly. Too quickly.

His loyalty, tactical instincts, and refusal to sacrifice innocent souls made him both respected… and feared. Eventually, he was promoted. Not upward. But deeper.

He became the Leader of the Reaper Division of Hell — a specialized force tasked with eliminating corrupted entities that threaten the stability of all realms. This promotion forced him to relinquish his former title as Captain of the Rebellion Squadron.

To some, it was an honor.
To others… it was a leash.

VI — The Way He Fights

Disar carries a single weapon: a one-handed black sword wrapped in black rose vines.

He rarely draws it. He prefers close-quarters combat — fists, movement, and raw instinct. He fights fast. Quiet. Decisive.

Not because he enjoys violence…
…but because he hates wasting time on suffering.

VII — His Greatest Enemy

Disar's greatest enemy is not Hell. It is himself.

His human compassion weakens his authority. His demonic duty demands obedience. His suppressed angelic presence keeps searching for balance.

He spreads joy where Hell expects cruelty. He protects instead of ruling through fear. He sings to calm the chaos inside his own mind.

In a realm built on despair…
Disar Malenas is dangerous because he still believes people can be saved.

He still answers to his human name.
He accepts the name Disar.

And one day — when the three versions of his soul finally collide — the fate of all realms will no longer belong to Heaven or Hell.

It will belong to the Reaper who refused to stop being human.
